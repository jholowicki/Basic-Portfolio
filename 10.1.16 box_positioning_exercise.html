Finance minister Arun Jaitley said the four-month window that ended on Friday brought in 64,275 declarations.All were offered immunity from prosecution in return for paying tax, a surcharge and a penalty.It is estimated that the government could raise nearly $4.5bn (£3.4bn) from the scheme.
